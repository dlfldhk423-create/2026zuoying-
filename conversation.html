認識彼此 (Getting to Know One Another)

我的名字
My name is...
你的名字
Your name?
你來自哪裡？
Where are you from?
你的家人有＿＿人？
How many people are in your family?
你怎麼認識真理？（挨家挨戶／非正式見證／家人／朋友）
How did you learn the truth? (House-to-house / Informal Witnessing / Family / Friends)
你的家人都是耶和華見證人嗎？
Is your whole family Jehovah's Witnesses?
什麼時候受浸？
When were you baptized?
你喜歡的經文是？
What's your favorite scripture?
你的會眾有幾個傳道員？幾個先驅？
How many publishers and pioneers are in your congregation?
很高興能跟你一起傳道！
It's nice to be in the ministry with you!

qǐng kàn duǎn piàn

hěn gāo xìng rèn shí nǐ

Can you eat spicy food? (sour / sweet / bitter / salty)

Do you like it?

很好吃！
It's delicious!

深入交流與分享 (Deep Conversations & Sharing)

A: 真高興見到您！
B: 我也是。
A: It’s really nice to see you！
B: Me too.
A: 您從哪裡來？
B: 大溪地, 加拿大, 印尼, 印度, 美國, 泰國, 索羅門群島, 馬來西亞, 斐濟, 斯里蘭卡, 菲律賓, 新喀里多尼亞, 緬甸
A: Where are you from?
B: Tahiti / Canada / Indonesia / India / the United States / Thailand / the Solomon Islands / Malaysia / Fiji / Sri Lanka / the Philippines / New Caledonia / Myanmar
A: 選擇台灣有特別的原因嗎？
B: 還沒來過台灣。
A: Is there a special reason you chose Taiwan?
B: I haven’t been to Taiwan before.
A: 台灣很熱，還習慣嗎？
B: 沒問題！還可以！
A: Taiwan is quite hot. Are you used to it?
B: No problem！It’s okay！
A: 台灣的主食是米 & 麵，西方的食物漢堡，炸雞，披薩 我們也很喜歡, 您的主食是什麼 ？
B: 其實跟你們的也差不多, 麵包、馬鈴薯、義大利麵，米飯，海鮮，培根，牛排....... 我們都接受。
A: In Taiwan, our staple foods are rice and noodles. We also like Western food like hamburgers, fried chicken and pizza. What are your staple foods?
B: They’re actually quite similar to yours—bread, potatoes, pasta, rice, seafood, bacon, steak... We eat all of these.
A: 有特別想去台灣哪裡看看嗎？
B: 想去台灣分部參觀。
A: Is there anywhere in Taiwan you especially want to visit?
B: I’d like to visit the Taiwan branch.
A: 想要吃什麼台灣美食？
B: 鼎泰豐、牛肉麵、鳳梨酥......也要去傳統市場看看水果。
A: What Taiwanese food would you like to try?
B: Din Tai Fung, beef noodles, pineapple cakes… I’d also like to go to traditional markets to see fruits.
A: 您是如何開始學習聖經的？
B:
1. 我是第 2 代見證人。
2. 我是傳道員來拜訪的。
3. 我的同事跟我傳道。
4. 我的阿姨是見證人。
A: How did you start studying the Bible?
B:
1. I am a second-generation Witness.
2. Someone came to preach to me.
3. My colleague preached to me.
4. My aunt is a Witness.
A: 學習聖經的過程有什麼阻礙呢 ？
B:
1. 我的家人屬於當地教會。
2. 當時我抽煙， 酗酒。
3. 我忙於工作。
4. 我的配偶是未信者。
A: What challenges did you face while studying the Bible?
B:
1. My family belongs to a local church.
2. I used to smoke and drink heavily.
3. I was busy with work.
4. My spouse is not a believer.
A: 您如何克服阻礙呢？
B:
1. 我常常禱告 , 沉思。
2. 弟兄姊妹不斷支持我。
3. 個人研讀的幫助很大。
A: How did you overcome those challenges?
B:
1. I often prayed and meditated.
2. The brothers and sisters kept supporting me.
3. Personal study helped me a lot.
A: 哪個經文最能感動您？
B: 啟示錄21：4
A: Which scripture has touched you the most?
B: Revelation 21:4
A: 最近有什麼屬靈計畫？
B: 加入中文會眾。
A: Do you have any recent spiritual goals?
B: To join a Chinese congregation.
A: 樂園的時候最想做什麼？
B: 蓋自己喜歡的房子。
A: What would you like to do the most in Paradise?
B: To build a house I like.
A: 有機會的話歡迎再來！
B: 一定會的。
A: You’re always welcome to visit again!
B: Thank you! I'd love to.
