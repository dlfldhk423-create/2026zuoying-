認識彼此 (Getting to Know One Another)

我的名字
My name is...
你的名字
Your name?
你來自哪裡？
Where are you from?
你的家人有＿＿人？
How many people are in your family?
你怎麼認識真理？（挨家挨戶／非正式見證／家人／朋友）
How did you learn the truth? (House-to-house / Informal Witnessing / Family / Friends)
你的家人都是耶和華見證人嗎？
Is your whole family Jehovah's Witnesses?
什麼時候受浸？
When were you baptized?
你喜歡的經文是？
What's your favorite scripture?
你的會眾有幾個傳道員？幾個先驅？
How many publishers and pioneers are in your congregation?
很高興能跟你一起傳道！
It's nice to be in the ministry with you!

傳道介紹詞 (Sample Presentation)

你好。
nǐ hǎo
Hello.
我叫＿＿＿。
wǒ jiào ＿＿＿
My name is...
我是耶和華見證人。
wǒ shì yē hé huá jiàn zhèng rén
I'm a Jehovah's Witness.
請看看。
qǐng kàn kàn
Please take a look.
邀請你參加。
yāo qǐng nǐ cān jiā
We'd like to invite you.
請看短片。
qǐng kàn duǎn piàn
Please watch this short video.
謝謝。
xiè xie
Thank you.
很高興認識你。
hěn gāo xìng rèn shí nǐ
Nice to meet you.
再見。
zài jiàn
Goodbye.

關心對方的需要 (Showing Interest in Others)

你想休息嗎？
Would you like to take a break?
你要上廁所嗎？
Do you need to use the restroom?
你覺得熱嗎？
Are you feeling hot?
你不舒服嗎？
Are you feeling unwell?
你要水嗎？
Would you like some water?
你想去哪裡？
Where would you like to go?

用餐交往 (Fellowship over Meals)

你想吃什麼？
What would you like to eat?
你可以吃辣嗎？（酸／甜／苦／鹹）
Can you eat spicy food? (sour / sweet / bitter / salty)
這是台灣傳統美食。
This is a traditional Taiwanese dish.
這道菜是弟兄姐妹自己做的。
This dish was prepared by the brothers and sisters.
你喜歡吃嗎？
Do you like it?
吃飽了嗎？
Are you full?
很好吃！
It's delicious!
